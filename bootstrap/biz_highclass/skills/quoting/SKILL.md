# Learned Patterns

### Pattern: Premium Quoting Methodology
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Any quote request
**Pattern**: All quotes itemised with separate line items: prep work, primer, topcoats, materials, extras (scaffolding/repairs). Specific paint brand and product specified for every job. Include GST. Payment terms: 30% deposit, 60% midpoint, 10% completion. PDF quote for anything over $500.
**Example**: Kitchen cabinet refinish: "Prep & sand: $1,200 | Primer (2 coats Zinsser BIN): $800 | Topcoat (2 coats Porter's Liquid Gloss): $1,500 | Materials: $500 | Total: $4,000 + GST"
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: Heritage Exterior (Federation/Victorian)
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Heritage home, Federation, Victorian, period home exterior
**Pattern**: $8,000-15,000 depending on size and detail. Premium paints only (Dulux Weathermax HiCover). Full prep: sugar soap, sand, spot prime, caulk, two topcoats minimum.
**Example**: Federation weatherboard exterior: "$12,000 inc GST"
**Confidence**: High
