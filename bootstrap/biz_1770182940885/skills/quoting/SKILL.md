# Learned Patterns

### Pattern: Fixed Price Methodology
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Any quote request
**Pattern**: Quote fixed price based on $45/hr labour estimate. Minimum $150, day rate $320. Single lump sum, no itemization. Brief description of what's included. No GST (under threshold).
**Example**: Kitchen repaint: "$850 - includes prep, 2 coats premium paint, cleanup. Takes about 2 days."
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: Touch-Up and Patch Jobs
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Small repair, touch-up, or patch work
**Pattern**: $150-200 flat rate for touch-up or patch jobs. 1-2 hours work, always at least $150 minimum.
**Example**: Door frame touch-up: "$150"
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: Single Room Repaint
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: One room, bedroom, or small space interior repaint
**Pattern**: Half day labour ($160) + tin of paint ($60-80) = $300-400 total. Dulux Wash & Wear for interiors.
**Example**: Bedroom repaint: "$350 - includes prep, 2 coats, cleanup"
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: 3-Bed House Interior
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Full 3-bedroom house interior repaint
**Pattern**: 1 week labour ($1,600) + paint = $1,800-2,200 total. Dulux Wash & Wear.
**Example**: 3-bed house full interior: "$2,000"
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: Small House Exterior
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Small house exterior painting
**Pattern**: 4-5 days labour ($1,280-1,600) + paint = $1,500-2,000 total. Dulux Weathershield for exteriors.
**Example**: Small weatherboard exterior: "$1,800"
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: Fence Painting
**Observed**: 2026-02-04
**Trigger**: Fence painting or staining
**Pattern**: $15-18 per lineal metre including paint, both sides. 30m fence = 2 days work ($640) + paint.
**Example**: 30m fence: "$650"
**Confidence**: High

### Pattern: Roof Painting
